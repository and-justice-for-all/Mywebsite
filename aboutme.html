Hobbies and interests

I spend most of my free time playing games, watching tv and movies, and reading comic books.

My favoirte movie is Blazing Saddles by Mel Brooks. It is a comedic satire of westerns that makes fun of the genre by adding somewhat more realistic elements, and making the more unbelievable aspects of westerns even more nonsensical.


























My favorite comic book series is Watchemen by Alan Moore. It is about the murder of a superhero and a vigilante detective who wants to find the killer. I like the series for it's satirical and surreal take on how superheroes would affect society.


























My favorite Video game series is Shin Megami Tensei, which is about killing demons for various different reasons.
